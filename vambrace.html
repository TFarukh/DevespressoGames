"...and bring the fire."

The Shade King has cursed the great city of Icenaire. Now cast under a permanent blight of frost fall, its former residents have returned from the dead as wraiths. Survivors take refuge deep under the city where they lead a desperate campaign against this unearthly power. Woefully outmatched, they are forced into hiding as the Shade King amasses an army of the undead above them. One fateful day, a mysterious stranger appears in the city with an enchanted vambrace. She may now be their only hope…
